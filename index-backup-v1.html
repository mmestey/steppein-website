FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is SteppeIn free for workers? +
Yes! SteppeIn is completely free for workers. Download the app, create your profile, and start applying to jobs. You'll never pay any fees.
How does same-day pay work? +
When you complete a job, employers can release payment immediately through the app. Funds are deposited directly to your linked bank account, debit card, or SteppeIn Cash Card.
What if I don't have a bank account? +
No problem! Get a free SteppeIn Cash Card, transfer to mobile wallets like Venmo or Cash App, or pick up cash at thousands of retail locations.
What types of jobs are available? +
SteppeIn features all types of agricultural work: harvest, packing, dairy, equipment operation, irrigation, nursery work, and more. Filter by job type to find what matches your skills.
How are employers verified? +
Every employer goes through our verification process including business license verification, identity confirmation, and agreement to our fair labor practices. We also collect worker reviews.
Is the app available in Spanish? +
Yes! SteppeIn is fully available in both English and Spanish. You can switch languages anytime in your settings.
